Did You Know?

 - Did
you know that the Federal Aviation Administration is Powered by Apache?
System-wide information management enables every airplane take off and
land in US airspace using Apache Camel and Apache ActiveMQ. https://projects.apache.org/projects.html?category

- Did you know that Lyft's real-time data querying and alerting is powered by Apache Druid (incubating), Flink, and Kafka? http://druid.apache.org/
