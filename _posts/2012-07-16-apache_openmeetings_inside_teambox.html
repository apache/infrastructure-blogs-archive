To add Apache Openmeetings for your organization in Teambox, select Manage Organizations → → Custom Apps (preview) → paste the following into the text field:

https://demo.dataved.ru/teambox/index.php?action=authorize, Openmeetings

This gives Teambox collaboration another advantage.
