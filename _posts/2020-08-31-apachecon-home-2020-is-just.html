We are 30 days out from the ApacheCon @Home event, and the ApacheCon Planners are busy pulling together a fantastic lineup of speakers, keynotes, and sponsor booths! 


Staging a virtual event has its own challenges, which you initially might not expect, compared with all the logistics of putting a real-world event together. After getting so many amazing talks and panel discussions, it quickly becomes clear that the limitations of time and space are no longer as big of a problem. 

Building the Schedule

At an in-person conference, you only have so many rooms and hours in the day. But now imagine a conference where there are no such limitations… attendees can visit sessions across many more tracks, a global schedule, and multiple languages. 


Interested in what’s going to happen? Take a look at our incredible schedule!

Keynote Speakers and Sponsors

Each day of ApacheCon @ Home 2020 is going to feature amazing speakers who will inspire and educate attendees! As we get closer to the event, we’ll have more information on the topics our speakers will be delivering, but take a look at who’s lined up to speak and get your planned agenda set!
