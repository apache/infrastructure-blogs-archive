Historically, The Apache Software Foundation has relied on volunteer effort to manage its administrative tasks. However, as the ASF has grown, the workload required, as well as our reliance on individual volunteers, has increased.

The Foundation is now seeking an executive assistant to lighten the burden on individual volunteers, as well as to provide consistent execution of tasks. Proposals from independent contractors are welcome.

The Request for Proposal and further details are available at http://www.apache.org/foundation/ASF-EA.html
