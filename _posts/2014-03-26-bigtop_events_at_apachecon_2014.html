Bigtop community will hold two events at the ApacheCon in Denver this year:

  1. April 7th, 6 pm: a meetup with talks about the technology, sharing the experience and socializing
  2. April 8th, 6 pm: a hackathon where we can focus on the technical issues and help new people to come up to speed with Bigtop framework.

Both events will take place in Teller Room, @ApacheCon, Westin Hotel, Denver

We are going to publish the agenda and list of speakers on our wiki page in the next a couple of days.

If you want to participate please RSVP via a twitter message with #asfbigtop or an email to user@bigtop.apache.org. The seating is limited, so hurry up!
