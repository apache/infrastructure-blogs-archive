At the Apache Software Foundation Board Meeting held this morning, the following members were elected to serve as the new Executive Officers of the ASF effective immediately:

- Doug Cutting, Chairman
- Jim Jagielski, President
- Greg Stein, Vice Chairman
- Noirin Shirley, Executive Vice President
- Geir Magnusson, Jr., Treasurer
- Craig Russell, Secretary
- Sam Ruby, Assistant Secretary

For more information on the Foundation's operations and its structure, see http://apache.org/foundation/how-it-works.html#structure
